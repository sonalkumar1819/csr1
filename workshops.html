Upcoming Workshops

Introduction to Satellite Technology

This workshop offers participants an overview of satellite systems, including their design, operation, and applications in various fields such as communication, weather forecasting, and remote sensing.

Data Analysis in Space Research

Participants will learn how to analyze and interpret space data using statistical tools and software. This hands-on workshop emphasizes practical skills for research and decision-making in space studies.

Sustainable Practices in Space Missions

This workshop explores sustainability in space exploration, discussing strategies to minimize environmental impacts, promote responsible resource use, and ensure long-term viability for space activities.

Career Paths in Space Science

Designed for students and young professionals, this workshop provides insights into various career opportunities in space science and technology, featuring guest speakers from academia and industry who share their experiences and advice.

Robotics in Space Exploration

Focusing on the role of robotics in space missions, this workshop covers the basics of robotic systems, their programming, and their application in planetary exploration and satellite maintenance.
