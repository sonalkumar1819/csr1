Welcome to my Home Page

The LPU-Centre for Space Research stands as a premier facility dedicated to fostering advanced research in space science and technology. Serving as a hub for aspiring technocrats and young researchers, this center provides a comprehensive platform for engaging in a range of activities, from satellite communication and data acquisition to engineering and scientific exploration. One of its key features is the ability to communicate with the International Space Station (ISS) and other satellites, allowing real-time downloading of satellite images and telemetry data from various spacecraft, including nano-satellites.

The primary objective of this center is to establish a world-class environment for the design and development of payloads for small satellite missions. It plays a vital role in space research at Lovely Professional University (LPU) while also acting as a virtual lab for other educational institutions across India and its neighboring countries. This initiative aims to enhance the capabilities of students, educators, and researchers in the dynamic field of space sciences.

Students associated with the center engage in a variety of hands-on activities such as satellite system design, subsystem development, satellite integration, and testing. They are involved in launching and operating satellites, processing payload data, and exploring diverse applications in space technology. The center also supports amateur radio (HAM) activities, participation in space-related competitions, and the networking of ground stations, which significantly contribute to scientific outreach and knowledge-sharing.
